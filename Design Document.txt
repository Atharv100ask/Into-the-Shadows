Game Idea:

Players must find the cure developed by Scientists to a Zombie Apocalypse caused by harmful radiation found in Sunlight
Players must stay in the shadows
Avoid direct Sunlight (carries harmful radiation which can turn you into a zombie over time)
Avoid / Kill Zombies 
Fighting Zombies alert nearby Zombies (gunfire, zombie grunts, etc.)
Find the cure before turning into a Zombie or Die Trying..

Target audience:
Survival Game lovers, Strategic Players
